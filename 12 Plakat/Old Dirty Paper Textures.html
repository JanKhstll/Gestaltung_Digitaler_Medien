This is an amazing texture pack: 15 high resolution grunge paper textures. The creative process behind this set is pretty funny: I had 15 parchments that I fold up and then merged into tea for 1 day. Once dried, I used acrylic colors to create nice details. At the end I used Photoshop to invert colors and apply some adjustments. I think the result is really cool. Enjoy them!
